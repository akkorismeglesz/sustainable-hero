Carbon Footprint Calculator

A carbon footprint calculator is a useful tool that helps individuals and organizations measure their environmental impact in terms of carbon emissions. By quantifying emissions from various activities, it raises awareness, sets reduction targets, and provides personalized recommendations for sustainability. It empowers users to make informed decisions and contribute to a more sustainable future.
